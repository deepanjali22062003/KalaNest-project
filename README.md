Local artisans and small market sellers (such as those in Banjara Market) create beautiful handcrafted ceramic and home décor items, but they face major challenges:

No digital presence or branding

Limited reach to customers beyond local markets

Dependence on footfall for sales

Low profit margins due to lack of direct access to buyers

At the same time, modern customers want unique, handmade, aesthetic home décor but:

Don’t know where to find authentic handcrafted products

Lack a single trusted online platform for such items

Face issues of quality, trust, and availability

This creates a gap between artisans/markets and digital customers.
Objectives
To design and develop a web-based platform for selling handcrafted ceramic and home décor items.

To provide a digital presence for locally sourced, handmade products.

To create a user-friendly interface for customers to browse, select, and purchase products easily.

To implement a secure authentication system for users and administrators.

To build an efficient product and inventory management system.

To integrate a secure online payment gateway for smooth transactions.

To manage the complete order lifecycle—from placing an order to delivery status.

To promote handmade and local craftsmanship through a modern digital medium.

To develop a scalable architecture that can be extended in the future into a multi-vendor platform.

To provide a real-world, industry-level full-stack project for academic and professional growth.
Proposed Solution
KalaNest is a web-based e-commerce platform that bridges this gap by:

Digitally showcasing handcrafted ceramic and home décor items

Allowing customers to browse, select, and purchase products online

Managing inventory, pricing, and orders through a centralized system

Integrating secure online payments

Providing a smooth, modern shopping experience

The platform follows a Direct-to-Customer (D2C) model where products are sourced from local markets and sold under a single digital brand. This:

Increases visibility of handcrafted items

Creates a sustainable business model

Promotes local craftsmanship

Offers customers aesthetic, authentic home décor
